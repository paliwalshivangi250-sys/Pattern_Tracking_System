Help & Information

Everything you need to know about the Pattern Tracking System.

How to Use
1

Select Your Symptoms

Click on the symptom cards that match what you're experiencing. You can select multiple symptoms.

2

Choose Your Location

Select which hostel or area of campus you are currently staying in.

3

Rate Severity

Indicate how severe your symptoms are — Mild, Moderate, or Severe.

4

Submit Anonymously

Click Submit Report. Your report is completely anonymous — no personal data is stored.

Privacy & Security
No name, student ID, or personal information is collected
Reports are stored anonymously in a secure database
Data is only used for campus health trend analysis
Your email is only stored locally in your browser session
When to Seek Medical Help

This system is for tracking patterns only. Please visit a doctor if you experience:

High fever above 103°F (39.4°C)
Difficulty breathing or chest pain
Symptoms lasting more than 3 days
Severe vomiting or inability to keep fluids down

Campus Health Center

For medical emergencies, contact your campus health center or call emergency services immediately.
